1. Wash and boil spinach (palak) leaves for 2–3 minutes. Cool and blend into a smooth puree.
2. In a pan, heat oil or ghee. Add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chillies. Sauté, then add tomatoes and cook till soft. Add spices (like turmeric, cumin, garam masala), then mix in the spinach puree.
3. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5–7 minutes. Add a little cream if you like. Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.
