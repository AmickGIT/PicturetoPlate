Dish: Toor Daal
Summary: 1. Rinse toor dal (split pigeon peas) well. Cook it in a pressure cooker or pot with water, turmeric, and salt until soft and mushy.
2. In a small pan, heat oil or ghee. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dried red chilies, chopped garlic, and curry leaves. Fry until fragrant.
3. Pour the tempering over the cooked dal. Mix well and simmer for a few minutes. Serve hot with rice or roti.
