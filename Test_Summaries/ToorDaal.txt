Dish: Toor Daal
Summary: 1. Rinse toor dal (split pigeon peas) thoroughly. Cook it with water, turmeric, and salt in a pressure cooker or pot until soft and creamy.
2. In a small pan, heat ghee or oil. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, dried red chilies, garlic, and a pinch of hing. Saut√© until golden and fragrant.
3. Pour this tempering over the cooked dal. Stir well, simmer briefly, and garnish with fresh coriander.
