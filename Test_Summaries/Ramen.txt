1. Cook instant or ramen noodles in boiling water as per packet instructions. Drain and keep aside.
2. In a pan, boil water or broth. Add chopped garlic, soy sauce, a little ginger, and veggies like carrots, spinach, or mushrooms. Cook until veggies are soft.
3. Add the cooked noodles to the soup. Top with a boiled egg, spring onions, or any extras you like.
