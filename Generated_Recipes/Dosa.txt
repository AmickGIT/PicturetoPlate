Title: Dosa

Dish Name: Dosa
Summary:
1. Combine rice and lentils to make batter. Heat oil in a pan and fry the batter until golden brown.
2.Idli batter mix with rice and Lentils. Heat in a pot and cook until fluffy.
3.Sambar is made with vegetables like potatoes, beans, and onions. Cook until thick. Serve with dosa and idli.
