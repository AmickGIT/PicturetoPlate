Title: Rice

Dish Name: Shrimp Fried Rice
Summary:
1. Cook rice according to package instructions. While it's cooking, slice shrimp into bite-sized pieces and chop vegetables like bell peppers, carrots and green onions.
2a. Heat oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add shrimp and vegetables and cook until shrimp is opaque and vegetables are softened.
2b. Add rice to the same pan and toss everything together until well coated with oil.
3a. Add any additional seasonings like soy sauce, oyster sauces, or sesame oil if desired.
3b. Serve immediately and enjoy!
