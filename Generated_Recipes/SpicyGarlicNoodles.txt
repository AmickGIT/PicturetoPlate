Title: Noodles

Dish Name: Stir-Fried Vegetables Noodles
Summary:

 A quick and easy Asian-inspired noodle and vegetable dish.

1. Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Add bell peppers and green beans; stir-fry for 3-4 minutes. Add noodles and stir-frying for another 3 minutes.
2.Add red bell peppers; toss for 4 minutes.
Add cilantro and serve immediately.
3.Don't forget to garnish with cilantro!
