Title: Daal

Dish Name: Daal

Summary:
1. Wash and soak the lentils overnight. Drain and rinse well.
2 Cook the lentil in water or broth with salt until tender. Add spices like cumin and coriader for flavor.
3 Blend the soup into a creamy consistency using a blender or food processor. Serve with naans or flatbreads for dipping.
