Title: Soupy Noodles

Dish Name: Spicy Ramen

Summary: Make a spicy noodle soup with meatballs and eggs.

1. In large pot, combine chicken stock, soy sauce, sesame oil, and chili flakes. Bring to boil over medium-high heat. Lower heat and simmer for about 1 minute. Add noodles and cook until tender, about 3 minutes. Drain noodles and return to pot.

2. Cut meatballs in half. Break eggs in half and place egg yolk on top of each egg white. Add meatballs to pot and gently stir to combine.

3. Garnish with chopped scallions, sesame seeds, and cilantro. Serve immediately.
