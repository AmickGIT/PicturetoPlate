Title: Italian

Dish Name: Penne Arrabdiata
Summary:
1. Cook penne in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2.
Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic and saute until fragrant. Add tomatoes and simmer for about 12 minutes. Season with salt.
3.
Add drained penne to the tomato sauce and toss to coat. Sprinkle with parsley and serve immediately.
