Title: Paneer

Dish Name: Paneer Paalak

Summary:

1. Wash and chop fresh spinach leaves into bite-sized pieces. or slice paneer into cubes. Heat some oil in a pan over medium heat, add the spinach and saute until wilted. Season with salt to taste.
2.Add the paneeri cubes to the spinach, toss together and cook until the paneers are golden brown and the spinach is wilted, remove from heat and serve.
3.Restaurants will often add some ghee or butter to the dish to give it a richer flavor.
